ABOUT US

Mommy & Wawa is a company that was inspired by the journey of a pregnancy of the founders and their experiences into parenthood, therefore they decided to create a place or platform where they could share, and source products for babies, toddlers and kids, that they struggled to find in the market, and place them on one platform for other parents to also enjoy, these includes educational products, toys, accessories and more of ours and our daughters favourite things. We also offer customizing services for our coloring books, journals and diaries, feel free to browse and enjoy our product offerings.
